Udupas Hospitality Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 14-06-2024
Our Commitment to Privacy

At Udupas Hospitality, we respect the privacy of our guests and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information. By using our services or accessing our website, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Guest Information: Name, contact details, date of birth, payment information, reservation details, special requests, and preferences.
  • Website Information: Information collected through cookies and similar technologies, such as IP address, browser type, operating system, referring website, pages viewed, and dates/times of visits.
  • Communication Information: Information provided when you contact us through email, phone, or other channels.
  • Social Media Information: Information collected from your social media interactions with us.
How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and Managing Services: To process reservations, provide accommodation, fulfill requests, and personalize your experience.
  • Improving Services: To analyze trends, gather feedback, and enhance our services.
  • Communication: To send you confirmations, updates, promotional offers, and respond to your inquiries.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations.
Disclosure of Your Information

We may share your personal information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party companies that provide services on our behalf, such as payment processing, marketing, and website hosting.
  • Business Partners: Partners with whom we offer joint promotions or services.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law Access and Correction: You may request access to or correction of your personal information.
  • Marketing Communications: You may opt-out of receiving marketing communications.
  • Cookies: You may manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.
Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes.
