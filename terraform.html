Welcome to Terraform

Terraform, managed by Udupas Hospitality, is a large event venue in North Bangalore. Designed to accommodate over 20,000 attendees, this 18-acre venue sets a new standard in South India for large-scale events, concerts, and corporate gatherings. It boasts state-of-the-art safety features, With state-of-the-art safety measures, luxurious amenities, and strategic proximity to Bangalore International Airport and major landmarks, Terraform ensures a seamless and memorable experience for all guests.
